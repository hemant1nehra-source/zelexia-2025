About the Event

LOSE YOURSELF
IN THE NEON

ZELEXIA is Thapar University's most electrifying DJ night — a full-scale neon rave experience inside the iconic Main Auditorium. Pulsating beats, laser lights, and a crowd that lives for the drop.

From opening acts to headline sets, Zelexia brings together the best of EDM, techno, and commercial dance music into one night you'll never forget. Dress to glow — neon encouraged, boring strictly prohibited.

Presented by Thapar University Student Bodies. Open to all Thapar students with valid college ID.
